Executive Summary

This document presents a comprehensive 6-month roadmap for implementing NebloBot, an AI-powered freight agent that integrates with broker/carrier TMS platforms and messaging platforms (WhatsApp and Telegram). The roadmap is divided into 6 phases with specific milestones, deliverables, and resource allocations to ensure successful implementation.

NebloBot aims to solve critical problems in the freight industry by automating load matching, capacity discovery, booking, and negotiation in real time. The implementation plan extends the original 3-month MVP timeline to a full 6-month plan that includes post-MVP features and ensures a robust, market-ready product.

This roadmap provides a structured approach to development, testing, and deployment, with clear milestones and deliverables for each phase. It also outlines the resources required for successful implementation, including human resources, technical infrastructure, and software tools.
